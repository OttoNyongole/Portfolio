About Me

I am Otto Nyongole, an Electronics and Telecommunication Engineer with 7 years of experience in the mobile communications industry. My career spans technical maintenance, network optimization, and system integration. Beyond telecom, I am a budding software developer exploring PHP, Python,HTML/Tailwind css, JavaScript, C, DevOps and MediaWiki customization, with a focus on building tools for knowledge sharing and farm management. Passionate about technology that drives real-world impact, I am currently developing digital solutions for sustainable pig farming and actively contributing to Wikimedia projects in Tanzania. My mission is to bridge engineering, open-source software, and agriculture to empower communities and create sustainable growth .
